Cognition and Computational Brain Lab

CUBRIC | Cardiff | United Kingdom

Welcome to the Cognition and Computational Brain Lab. We are based in the Cardiff University Brain Research Imaging Centre.

Our lab is focused on the neural and computational mechanisms of decision-making, learning and action. A central theme is to understand how the human brain integrates cognitive and perceptual processes to regulate behaviour in a dynamic environment. New insights on these basic cognitive operations help us to understand cognitive deficits in neurodegeneration and dementia. We use a combination of multimodal brain imaging, behavioural measures, and computational modelling.

Our current research is funded by the European Research Council and the Medical Research Council. We are grateful for their support.
