Cognition and Computational Brain Lab

Swansea | Cardiff | United Kingdom

Welcome to the Cognition and Computational Brain Lab. We are based in the Swansea University Computational Foundry and Cardiff University Brain Research Imaging Centre.

Our lab focuses on human and machine intelligence. We combines computational modelling, machine learning, brain imaging, and experimental approaches to understand human cognition, ageing, and cognitive deficits in neurological and psychiatric disorders. On machine intelligence, we are interested in developing artificial agents to mimic the human capacity of complex cognitive operation and social interaction.
